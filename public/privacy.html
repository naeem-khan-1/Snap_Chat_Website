Privacy Policy — SnapSaver

Last updated: June 23, 2026

At thesnapsaver.com, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it secure. Please read this policy carefully before using our website. If you have any questions, contact us at contact@thesnapsaver.com.

1. General Overview

SnapSaver is an independent online tool that allows users to view and download public Snapchat videos, stories, highlights and spotlights. We do not require you to create an account or submit personal data to use our services. SnapSaver operates in compliance with applicable data protection and privacy laws.

2. Personal Identification Information

SnapSaver does not actively collect or store personally identifiable information such as your name, email address or phone number. Users may visit our website anonymously. If you voluntarily submit any data (for example through a contact email), it will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared with third parties.

3. Non-Personal Identification Information

We may collect limited non-personal data about users automatically when they interact with thesnapsaver.com. This may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Referring website or search query
  • Time spent on the website and pages visited
  • Anonymous analytics for performance monitoring

This data helps us improve site performance and user experience. It does not personally identify you.

4. Cookies

SnapSaver uses cookies to ensure the proper functionality of the website and improve user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device for technical, statistical or advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling cookies may limit certain features on our site.

5. How We Use Collected Information

SnapSaver does not collect, log or store any URLs, videos or downloaded data from users. All operations are processed in real time and automatically deleted after completion. We do not track your activities, maintain download logs or build usage profiles.

Any non-personal technical data (for example anonymous analytics) is used only to:

  • Improve website functionality and speed
  • Analyze general trends and detect errors
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

6. Advertising and Third-Party Services

SnapSaver displays advertisements served by third-party ad networks, including Google AdSense, to support our free service. These partners may use cookies to deliver relevant ads and measure performance. These cookies do not include personally identifiable information.

This Privacy Policy does not cover third-party advertisers' practices. Please review their respective privacy policies for more information on how they handle data.

7. Google AdSense

Some ads may be served by Google. Google's use of advertising cookies (including the DART cookie) allows it to serve ads to users based on their visits to our site and other sites across the internet. DART uses non-personally identifiable information and does not track personal data such as your name, email address or physical address.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

8. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical measures to safeguard data transmitted through our website. However, no online transmission or storage method is completely secure. SnapSaver does not store or retain any user-generated content, links or downloads after processing.

9. Third-Party Links

thesnapsaver.com may contain links to third-party websites for user convenience. We are not responsible for the content, privacy policies or practices of those sites. Visiting any linked websites is at your own risk.

10. Children's Privacy

SnapSaver is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

SnapSaver reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When changes are made, the updated date will appear at the top of this page. Users are encouraged to check this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

12. Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using thesnapsaver.com, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website. Continued use after changes are posted will be deemed acceptance of the updated policy.
